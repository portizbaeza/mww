OUR VISION

The mountain unites us. United, equal, supporting and caring for each other to tell a collective feminist hiking story.

OUR GOAL

To create a community worldwide organization working for local mountain women development, fostering women empowerment, community-based tourism, capacity building, and sustainable innovative solutions.

We aim to …

… bring mountain women together

… learn and share the experiences of each othe

… provide guidance, support, and partnerships, through knowledge sharing.

… support women led mountain organizations, environment sustainability projects, training and sustainability

… share tools and resources about mountain protection and sustainable mountain activities.
